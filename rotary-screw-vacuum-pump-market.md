<p><p>The Rotary Screw Vacuum Pump market is categorized into air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled pumps utilize ambient air for cooling, making them suitable for applications with limited water supply or where water contamination could be an issue. They are typically more compact and easier to install. In contrast, water-cooled pumps rely on circulating water for heat dissipation, allowing for higher efficiency and extended operation times in demanding environments. Each type addresses specific operational needs and installation conditions in industrial applications.</p></p>
