<p><ul><li>Permanent-magnet Demagnetizer</li><li>Stored-energy Demagnetizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipeline demagnetizer market includes two main types: permanent-magnet demagnetizers and stored-energy demagnetizers. Permanent-magnet demagnetizers utilize strong magnetic fields generated by permanent magnets to eliminate unwanted magnetism in pipeline components. In contrast, stored-energy demagnetizers use an electrical energy storage mechanism to produce a short but intense magnetic field that disrupts residual magnetism. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the integrity and functionality of pipelines by preventing magnetic interference and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
