<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dual Band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual-band wireless router market features several standards, each catering to different user needs. 802.11a operates in the 5 GHz band with faster speeds but shorter range. 802.11b uses the 2.4 GHz band, offering greater range but lower speeds. 802.11g combines the best of both worlds with improved speed and range while still using 2.4 GHz. 802.11n enhances performance through MIMO technology, and 802.11ac provides even higher speeds and efficient bandwidth usage in the 5 GHz band, ideal for streaming and gaming.</p></p>
