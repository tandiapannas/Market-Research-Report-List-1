<p><p>The propeller anemometer market can be categorized primarily into two types: Trefoil and Yotsuba. The Trefoil anemometer features three blades that efficiently measure airflow speed, making it suitable for various environmental monitoring applications. In contrast, the Yotsuba anemometer, with its unique four-blade design, provides enhanced stability and accuracy in turbulent conditions, catering to specialized industrial sectors. Both types are widely used for assessing air movement in HVAC systems, meteorology, and research applications, highlighting their significance in environmental assessments.</p></p>
