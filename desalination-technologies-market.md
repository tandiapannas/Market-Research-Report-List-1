<p><p>Desalination technologies primarily include Reverse Osmosis (RO), Multi-stage Filtration (MSF), Multi-effect Distillation (MED), and other methods. RO utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate salt from water, offering high efficiency and energy savings. MSF involves heating seawater, then using an array of chambers to create freshwater through condensation. MED employs multiple evaporators to maximize efficiency and minimize energy use. Other methods may include electro-dialysis and solar desalination, catering to varying needs in different geographical and economic contexts.</p></p>
