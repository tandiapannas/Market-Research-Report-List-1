<p><p>Wearable bio sensors for healthcare monitoring can be categorized into two main market types: single-use and repeated-use systems. Single-use sensors are designed for one-time applications, providing immediate data for specific situations, such as continuous glucose monitoring during a medical event. In contrast, repeated-use sensors are more versatile, allowing for long-term health tracking and management. They often feature rechargeable batteries and durable materials, enabling consistent monitoring of vital signs and other health indicators over extended periods, facilitating ongoing patient care.</p></p>
