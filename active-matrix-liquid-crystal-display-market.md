<p><p>Active matrix liquid crystal display (AMLCD) technology is a flat-panel display technology that employs a matrix of pixels, each controlled by a thin-film transistor (TFT). AMLCDs are widely used in a variety of applications, including televisions, computer monitors, smartphones, and tablets.</p><p>The Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display (AMLCD) market features two primary types: Polycrystalline Silicon (p-Si) and Amorphous Silicon (a-Si). Polycrystalline silicon offers higher electron mobility, enabling faster refresh rates and improved image quality, making it ideal for high-resolution displays, such as televisions and monitors. In contrast, amorphous silicon, characterized by lower production costs and simpler manufacturing processes, is commonly used in smaller devices like smartphones and tablets. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>
