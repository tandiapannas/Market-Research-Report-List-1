<p><ul><li>Thermal Expansion Valve</li><li>Thermostatic Expansion Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning expansion valve market consists primarily of two types: thermal expansion valves and thermostatic expansion valves. Thermal expansion valves regulate refrigerant flow based on temperature changes, optimizing cooling efficiency. They operate by sensing the evaporator temperature and adjusting flow accordingly. On the other hand, thermostatic expansion valves utilize a sensor bulb that directly responds to the refrigerant pressure, maintaining a steady flow and enhancing system performance. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring effective temperature control in HVAC systems.</p></p>
