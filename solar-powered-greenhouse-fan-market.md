<p><p>The solar-powered greenhouse fan market can be categorized into two main types: circular and exhaust fans. Circular fans are designed to distribute air evenly throughout the greenhouse, promoting uniform temperature and humidity levels, which enhances plant growth. In contrast, exhaust fans focus on removing hot, stale air from the greenhouse, replacing it with cooler, fresher air from outside. Both types utilize solar energy, making them eco-friendly and cost-effective solutions for maintaining optimal growing conditions in a greenhouse environment.</p></p>
