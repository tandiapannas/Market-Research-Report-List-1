<p><p>The Arc Welding Inverter market encompasses various welding techniques, each suited for specific applications. MMA (Manual Metal Arc) is a versatile process ideal for outdoor work and thicker materials. MIG/MAG (Metal Inert Gas/Metal Active Gas) is favored for its speed and ease of use, making it suitable for thin materials and production settings. TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) offers precision and control, commonly used for welding non-ferrous metals. Other types include sub-arc and plasma arc welding, catering to specialized industrial needs.</p></p>
