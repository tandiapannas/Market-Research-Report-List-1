<p><p>The ballistic helmet market comprises three primary types based on material: metal, nonmetal, and composite. Metal helmets, often made of steel or titanium, offer high-level protection but can be heavy. Nonmetal helmets, typically constructed from advanced polymers, focus on lightweight design and good ballistic resistance while enhancing comfort. Composite helmets blend materials, such as aramid fibers and ceramics, to provide optimal protection, reduced weight, and better performance. Each type caters to diverse needs in military, law enforcement, and security sectors.</p></p>
